# Weekly Report of Team 7
## Weekly Report - Week of December 4, 2023
1. Introduction:

    Great news this week – we've successfully implemented the maze. It's a significant step forward for the whole team. Building on this success, we've begun working on implementing Pac-Man's collision with walls.
2. Accomplishments:
- Successfully completed the maze.
3. Challenges:
- Adjusting the speed relationship between Pac-Man and the collision check-update.
- Incorporating the presence of four ghosts into the maze.
4. Plans for Next Week:
- Finalize the implementation of Pac-Man's collision with walls.
- Integrate at least one or more ghosts into the maze.
- Implement collision detection for the ghosts.
- Introduce movement patterns for the ghosts.
5. Conclusion:
Exciting progress with the maze completion! The upcoming tasks, especially fine-tuning Pac-Man's collision and introducing ghosts with their movements, promise to bring more dynamism to the game. Looking forward to another productive week!
